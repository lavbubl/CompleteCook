Binds:
Complete Cook version HUD - obj_camera
Player reset position & Restart game - obj_player

Commands:
room_goto Room Door(a,b,c,d,e,f,g)		- Same as room_goto function in gml, however you can now choose the door
togglecollisions true/false		- Toggle collision visibility
panic active:true/false time:number		- Sets your Pizza Time and Pizza Time left time
set_combo amount:number time:number		- Sets your combo and combo left time
noclip     - Makes your player able to fly. Shift to speed up. Jump to leave noclip.
